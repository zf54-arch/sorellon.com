Modern Slavery and Human Trafficking Statement

This statement is made pursuant to section 54 of the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and sets out the steps Sorellon Limited has taken to prevent modern slavery and human trafficking within its business and supply arrangements.

1. Organisation

Sorellon Limited is a UK registered company providing business to business procurement and supply services. The company operates primarily in the United Kingdom and supplies goods and services to public sector and commercial customers.

2. Policy Commitment

Sorellon Limited is committed to acting ethically and with integrity in all business relationships and to implementing appropriate measures to reduce the risk of modern slavery and human trafficking.

3. Risk Assessment

The nature of Sorellon Limited’s activities means that the risk of modern slavery within its direct operations is considered low. Risk is assessed on a proportionate basis having regard to the scale and nature of the business.

4. Due Diligence

Sorellon Limited undertakes reasonable and proportionate due diligence measures appropriate to its operations. These measures are designed to identify and mitigate potential risks of modern slavery and human trafficking where practicable.

5. Training and Awareness

Awareness of ethical business conduct and compliance obligations is maintained at management level. Formal training is implemented where appropriate to the size and structure of the business.

6. Effectiveness

Sorellon Limited keeps its approach under review and will update this statement as appropriate to reflect changes in operations, regulation, or guidance.

7. Approval

This statement has been approved by the management of Sorellon Limited and is reviewed annually.

8. Legal Notice

This statement is provided for transparency and compliance purposes only. It does not constitute a contractual obligation, warranty, or representation.

  • Sorellon Limited makes no guarantees regarding third party practices.
  • This statement reflects the position at the date of publication.
  • To the maximum extent permitted by law, Sorellon Limited accepts no liability arising from reliance on this statement.

Nothing in this statement limits liability for fraud or for death or personal injury caused by negligence.
