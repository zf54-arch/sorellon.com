Carbon Reduction Plan

This Carbon Reduction Plan sets out Sorellon Limited’s approach to managing and reducing carbon emissions in accordance with applicable UK public sector procurement requirements.

1. Commitment

Sorellon Limited is committed to reducing the environmental impact of its operations where practical and proportionate to the nature and scale of its business activities.

2. Scope

This plan applies to activities undertaken by Sorellon Limited in the delivery of goods and services to customers. The scope of emissions considered reflects the operational boundaries of the business.

3. Baseline

A baseline position has been established for internal reference purposes. Where required, emissions are assessed using reasonable estimates based on available activity data and recognised industry methodologies.

4. Emissions Management

Sorellon Limited seeks to manage emissions through appropriate operational controls and procurement practices. Actions are reviewed periodically and adjusted in line with business activity and regulatory guidance.

5. Carbon Reduction Measures

Carbon reduction measures are implemented where feasible and commercially reasonable, with consideration given to efficiency, reliability, and compliance requirements.

6. Governance

Responsibility for this Carbon Reduction Plan rests with the management of Sorellon Limited. The plan is reviewed and updated as necessary to reflect changes in regulation or business operations.

7. Compliance Statement

This Carbon Reduction Plan is published to support compliance with UK public sector procurement regulations and related policy guidance.
