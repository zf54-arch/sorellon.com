Sorellon Limited — Capability Statement

Company No. 16575318 · England & Wales · Public Sector Supply

About Us

Sorellon Limited is a UK-based public sector supply company delivering reliable, value-for-money goods to government, healthcare, and education buyers. We specialise in compliant sourcing, transparent pricing, and fast fulfilment — designed for organisations that value audit readiness and dependable service.

Our approach is simple: straightforward products, serious delivery. Every order is managed by a named UK contact with full accountability from enquiry to delivery, supported by a responsive global supply chain and a strict focus on documentation accuracy and performance reporting.

Due to confidentiality agreements and public sector procurement protocols, Sorellon does not publicly disclose individual client names. However, our team has experience fulfilling orders within NHS Trusts, local authorities, and education frameworks through both direct supply and subcontracted partnerships.

Mission & Values

  • Integrity: Honest pricing, traceable sourcing, and open communication.
  • Responsiveness: Quotes within 24 hours, status updates throughout fulfilment.
  • Reliability: On-time delivery and full QA documentation with every order.
  • Accountability: A named UK contact — no call centres, no delays.

Core Capabilities

1) Strategic Sourcing & Procurement Fit

  • Direct access to audited manufacturers in the UK, EU, and China.
  • Procurement alignment with NHS Supply Chain, CCS, and local authority frameworks.
  • Product research and technical data collation for tender compliance.
  • Supplier due diligence, ethical sourcing verification, and sustainability checks.

2) Compliance & Quality Assurance

  • Conformity with ISO 9001 (Quality Management) and ISO 14001 (Environmental).
  • Supply documentation including CE/UKCA marking, MSDS, and CoC certification.
  • Complete traceability from factory to end-user delivery.
  • Batch testing, sample retention, and inspection reports on request.

3) Logistics & Fulfilment

  • UK warehousing and consolidation for multi-line orders.
  • Air and sea freight import management, customs clearance, and duties handled.
  • Stockholding and scheduled call-off options for recurring contracts.
  • GPS-tracked delivery and POD integration for contract compliance.

4) Contract Readiness & Procurement Experience

  • Experienced in open procedure, request-for-quotation, and framework call-off models.
  • Flexible on terms: standard 30-day payment, longer for framework authorities.
  • Cyber Essentials-ready systems and secure data handling for portal submissions.
  • Fully registered on UK public procurement portals (Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, and others).

5) Product Categories

  • Medical & Hygiene: Disposable wipes, syringes, catheters, gloves, disinfectants, PPE.
  • Facilities & Janitorial: Cleaning kits, bin liners, mops, buckets, floor consumables.
  • Education & Office: Stationery, teaching aids, storage solutions, safety products.
  • Infrastructure & Trade: Paint kits, tools, tapes, protective coatings, light consumables.
  • Sustainability Line: Eco-friendly wipes, biodegradable packaging, and low-carbon shipping options.

Added Value Services

  • Custom packaging and private labelling for framework or brand compliance.
  • Consolidated invoicing for simplified procurement processing.
  • On-demand technical datasheets and sample submission for evaluation.
  • Product substitution and equivalent matching to meet specification or budget targets.

Key Differentiators

  • Public Sector Focus: Built exclusively for institutional buyers.
  • Fast Turnaround: 24-hour quote response, 5–10 day air freight, 25–35 day sea freight.
  • Audit Ready: Full document trail from factory to final invoice.
  • Low Overheads: Lean structure means competitive, fixed pricing.
  • Proven Partnerships: Verified manufacturers with consistent QC results.

Sustainability & Social Value

  • Supporting the UK government's net zero and local growth agenda.
  • Partnering with logistics providers offering carbon offset delivery.
  • Prioritising recyclable and biodegradable materials where viable.
  • Promoting ethical labour and fair-trade practices in supplier selection.
  • Supporting small British manufacturers through blended supply models.

Our Promise

  • Rapid Response: Quotes within 24 hours.
  • Transparency: Clear, fixed pricing and open communication.
  • Reliability: End-to-end control from sourcing to delivery.
  • Compliance: Full documentation for audit and inspection.
  • Accountability: Named UK contact throughout every project.
